Mobile Solutions

The world is going mobile. On top of all of the features listed below the brokerage offering mobile access to their clients data on mobile will automatically get more exposure online.

Due to the nature of mobile presence you get new points of entry: Google Play Store and Apple App Store. This allows you to advertise you Brokerage for the entire new market as well as receive the SEO benefit of a back link from apple and google.

Overview of all accounts with balances, account types, trade history.

Allow your cients to access the trade history without having them being tied to their desk. Everyone who ever traided had a desire to share their experience with others. Not only it boosts their confidence it also promotes your brand.

Mobile Deposits

Streamline the ability to receive mobile deposits weather it is from a credit card or via bank transfer. The app allows your customers to see your bank details for transfers while on the go,making sure they always have that information in their pocket.
